About AU

Barbara Kingsolver once famously said that, “No other continent has endured such an unspeakably bizarre combination of foreign thievery and foreign goodwill.” The African Union is Africa's inspiring step toward creating an Africa that stands proud in front of its global counterparts. 54 countries come together to form the African Union to decide and take action on steps that range from improving territorial integrity to marked improvement in science and technology. The AU is and will be instrumental in shaping up Africa by wielding inter-dependence and co-operative growth to ensure a better future for Africa. Here at SSNMUN 2016 join us as we simulate a committee that seeks to address issues that are pivotal for Africa's deservedly brighter tomorrow. "Sien julle hier".
